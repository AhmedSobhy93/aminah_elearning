Our Mission

At Aminah Hospital Benha, our mission is to provide exceptional medical care, cutting-edge research, and accessible education to healthcare professionals and students. We aim to bridge the gap between clinical excellence and continuous learning through our innovative e-learning platform.

About Dr. Ahmed Saber

Dr. Ahmed Saber, the medical director of Aminah Hospital Benha, is a distinguished healthcare professional with extensive experience in clinical medicine and digital medical education. His vision has driven the integration of technology into medical learning, ensuring every practitioner can access quality training and certification opportunities.

Under his leadership, Aminah Hospital has become a hub for continuous professional development, combining patient care with innovative e-learning initiatives.
